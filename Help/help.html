What is Sangeet?

Sangeet is a digital music, podcast, and video service that gives you access to songs and other content from creators. Basic functions such as playing music are totally free, but you can also choose to upgrade to Spotify Premium. Whether you have Premium or not, you can

  • Get recommendations based on your taste
  • Build collections of music and podcasts
And more!
---------------------------------
Sangeet is available across a range of devices, including computers, phones, tablets, speakers, TVs, and cars, and you can easily transition from one to another with Sangeet Connect.
Can I keep music from Sangeet?
Our licensing means there's no way to export our content outside of the app.
